LEO – Enterprise AI Orchestration Platform

LEO is an enterprise AI orchestration platform designed to enable agent-driven execution across SAP and adjacent enterprise systems. It brings together runtime orchestration, semantic retrieval, tool access, and governed execution patterns to support intelligent business workflows.

SAP Analytics Decision Intelligence SAC S/4HANA

Enterprise Analytics and Decision Intelligence

Problem: Reporting was fragmented, manual, and disconnected from real-time operations — limiting the speed and quality of business decisions.

Designed and implemented an SAP Analytics Cloud decision intelligence layer spanning production, procurement, logistics, and commercial functions. The programme focused on replacing fragmented reporting with live dashboards, predictive models, and more responsive business decision-making.

  • Projected annual value potential exceeding $7M through yield-related insights
  • Faster reporting cycles and reduced manual reporting effort
  • Live SAP Analytics Cloud dashboards for cross-functional visibility
