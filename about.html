About

From Enterprise Systems to Intelligent Enterprises

A digital transformation leader working at the intersection of SAP, AI, analytics, and enterprise architecture.

I am a digital transformation leader working at the intersection of SAP, AI, analytics, and enterprise architecture. Over the past two decades, I have worked across manufacturing, supply chain, operations, and enterprise technology to design and deliver transformation initiatives with real business impact.

My work focuses on bridging the gap between enterprise systems and intelligent execution. That means going beyond workflow automation to embed reasoning, insight, and adaptive decision support into the way organizations operate.

My portfolio spans SAP-led business transformation, enterprise AI platforms, agent-based systems, decision intelligence, operational analytics, and sustainability-oriented AI solutions.

I combine technical depth with business and transformation thinking. I hold a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence and have completed the Chief Digital and AI Officer programme at the Indian School of Business, which strengthened my ability to connect enterprise strategy, operating models, and intelligent systems.

The goal is consistent across everything I work on: move enterprises from static, process-driven systems to intelligent, adaptive organizations.
