P Problem

Organizations face mounting pressure to produce accurate, timely ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) reporting. However, ESG data is notoriously fragmented — spread across PDF reports, spreadsheets, regulatory filings, and third-party databases. Manual collection and analysis is slow, error-prone, and fails to scale with increasing regulatory complexity and stakeholder expectations.

S Solution

As Strategic AI Advisor, I shaped the AI architecture and product direction of Ecodrisil — an AI-driven ESG intelligence platform that automates the full lifecycle of ESG data, from ingestion to actionable insight. The platform is commercially available at ecodrisil.com. Key capabilities include:

  • → Automated ingestion of ESG reports, regulatory documents, and sustainability disclosures from multiple sources
  • → RAG-based querying — users can ask natural language questions directly against ingested ESG documents
  • → NLP/NLQ interface for interactive ESG analytics without requiring technical expertise
  • → ESG benchmarking across industry frameworks (GRI, TCFD, CSRD) and peer comparison
  • → Analytics dashboards tracking ESG KPIs over time with trend analysis and gap identification
