P Problem

Organizations face mounting regulatory pressure to produce accurate, timely ESG reports — but ESG data is notoriously fragmented across spreadsheets, PDFs, operational systems, and third-party databases. Manual collection is slow and error-prone, carbon accounting is complex across Scope 1, 2, and 3 boundaries, and sustainability teams lack the tools to interact with their own data conversationally. The result is delayed disclosures, compliance risk, and strategic blind spots.

R My Role

Engaged as Strategic AI Advisor on a pro-bono basis, I shaped the AI architecture and product intelligence of Ecodrisil's ESG Express platform. My focus was on the ESGAI® Copilot layer — the conversational AI, document intelligence, and automated reporting capabilities that differentiate the platform from traditional ESG software. I worked closely with the product team to ensure the AI layer was grounded in real ESG frameworks (GRI, DEFRA) and enterprise data realities.

S Platform Capabilities

📊 ESG Performance Dashboard

A unified real-time view of environmental KPIs — GHG emissions, water consumption, waste generation, and renewable energy usage — all tracked over time with trend indicators.

  • → Scope 1/2/3 emissions tracked across upstream, capital goods, fuel, and energy activities
  • → Water consumption and recycling metrics with year-on-year trending
  • → Renewable energy breakdown — geothermal, solar, and wind — across multiple years
  • → Waste generation and non-recycled waste monitoring

✦ ESGAI® Copilot – AI-Powered Reporting Assistant

The platform's standout capability: an AI copilot that validates ESG responses, auto-drafts full ESG reports, and enables users to chat conversationally with their own sustainability data.

  • → Auto-draft ESG reports — copilot generates report sections from collected data, flagging gaps with guided hints
  • → Response validation — AI evaluates the quality and completeness of ESG disclosures in real time
  • → Chat with Data (Beta) — natural language queries against ESG data (e.g. "Plot fuel consumption for 2022 and 2023")
  • → Document Analysis — upload files or URLs; ESGAI® automatically searches and extracts relevant ESG data

🌍 Carbon Emission Calculation Engine

A granular, standards-aligned carbon accounting module covering all three emission scopes with DEFRA emission factor tables.

  • → Scope 1: Stationary combustion, mobile combustion, and fugitive emissions
  • → Multi-level source classification (fuel type → energy type → specific fuel → quantity)
  • → Auto-computed CO2, CH4, and N2O breakdowns with total CO2e output
  • → DEFRA-aligned emission tables with configurable emission factors
