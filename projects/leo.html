LEO – Enterprise AI Orchestration Platform

An AI-native orchestration layer designed to bring agentic intelligence into enterprise systems, particularly SAP landscapes. LEO introduces an agent layer that can reason, act, and interact with enterprise data and services.

The Problem

SAP Lacks Native Agentic Intelligence

SAP enterprises lack an AI-native orchestration layer to integrate large language models, enterprise data, and existing workflows into a unified intelligent system. Business processes remain siloed, and the path from raw enterprise data to actionable AI-driven outcomes requires significant custom development for each use case — slowing adoption and limiting scalability.

Every exception requires human intervention. Every decision depends on someone pulling a report. There is no layer that can reason, prioritize, and act on enterprise data dynamically.

Use Cases

What LEO Enables

Automated Invoice Validation

Agent-driven three-way matching across purchase orders, goods receipts, and supplier invoices — with exception handling and SAP posting.

Intelligent Procurement Workflows

Agentic sourcing support — supplier comparison, contract retrieval, approval routing, and SAP requisition creation.

Document Understanding and Action

Extract structured information from contracts and purchase documents, then act on them through SAP-connected workflows.
