The Problem

Fragmented Data, Delayed Decisions

The enterprise was operating with fragmented data and no unified visibility across production, procurement, HR, and logistics. Reporting relied on manual consolidation from multiple SAP modules, decision-making lagged behind operational reality by days, and there was no mechanism to predict yield loss before it materialized on the production line.

Senior leadership had no real-time picture of commercial performance vs. targets, and procurement teams lacked supplier performance visibility to inform sourcing decisions.

Solution

Four Purpose-Built Decision Dashboards

ProductionPredictive

Yield Performance Analytics

Multi-tab SAC dashboard tracking production yield, raw material quality, by-product ratios, and annual trend analysis. Predictive analytics enable proactive intervention before yield loss occurs.

Primary source of the $7M+ projected annual value potential.
