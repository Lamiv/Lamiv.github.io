Enterprise Analytics & AI Intelligence

Built a unified analytics intelligence layer across a large continuous manufacturing enterprise — replacing fragmented manual reporting with real-time SAP Analytics Cloud dashboards, predictive yield optimization, procurement intelligence, and commercial performance analytics.

Problem

The enterprise was operating with fragmented data and no unified visibility across production, procurement, HR, and logistics. Reporting relied on manual consolidation from multiple SAP modules, decision-making lagged behind operational reality by days, and there was no mechanism to predict yield loss before it materialized on the production line. Senior leadership had no real-time picture of commercial performance vs. targets.

Analytics Architecture

Implemented SAP Analytics Cloud as the intelligence layer across all business functions, backed by ELT pipelines pulling from S/4HANA, manufacturing execution systems, and logistics data. Four purpose-built dashboards, each targeting a specific decision domain:

📊 Yield Performance & Refinery Intelligence

Six-tab SAC dashboard tracking production yield, raw material quality, by-product ratios, and annual trend analysis. Predictive analytics enable proactive intervention before yield loss occurs — the primary source of the $7M+ projected annual savings.

Real-time logistics visibility dashboard monitoring truck cycle times, loading throughput, and dispatch performance across all product lines and shipping types. Enabled operations management to identify bottlenecks and balance load across dispatch channels.
