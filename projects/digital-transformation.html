Context

The Problem

A large continuous manufacturing enterprise was running HR on paper-based and legacy workflows, selling through telephone-based ordering with no digital channel, and coordinating field logistics through manual dispatch. These processes were slow, error-prone, and increasingly inadequate as the business scaled.

There was no unified digital layer connecting enterprise systems with field operations or customer channels. Every exception required manual intervention. Management had no real-time visibility into workforce metrics, order pipeline, or delivery status.

Transformation Programs

Three Interdependent Programs Delivered in Parallel

HR TransformationSAP SuccessFactors

HR Transformation and Digitization

Led the HR digital transformation, replacing paper-based and legacy HR processes with integrated SAP SuccessFactors workflows across the full employee lifecycle — from onboarding to performance and payroll.

  • SAP SuccessFactors implementation — core HR, onboarding, and performance management
  • Digitization of HR records, approvals, and employee self-service workflows
  • Integration with payroll and attendance management systems
  • HR analytics dashboards surfacing workforce metrics and compliance data
EcommerceSAP S/4HANABTPCPI

Ecommerce Platform × SAP S/4HANA Integration

Designed and architected the integration between a new ecommerce platform and SAP S/4HANA using modern integration patterns via SAP BTP and CPI. Built around the operational realities of manufacturing — not just generic ecommerce.

  • Capacity-driven order acceptance — orders accepted or queued based on live production capacity, not just stock
  • Multi-SKU and packaging type support with real-time S/4HANA inventory resolution
  • Automated order confirmation, delivery scheduling, and SAP posting via CPI integration flows
  • Customer-facing visibility into order status and delivery tracking
Field LogisticsMobile AppOperations

Driver Mobile App for Field Logistics

Developed a purpose-built mobile application to replace manual dispatch and paper-based driver operations, connecting field logistics with SAP delivery orders and real-time operational data.

  • Route optimization and scheduling integration with SAP delivery management
  • Real-time status updates and proof-of-delivery workflows
  • Automated mileage, load, and trip logging replacing manual dockets
  • Dispatcher visibility into driver status, location, and delivery progress
Outcomes

Business Impact

The three programs collectively eliminated the majority of manual data entry, paper records, and disconnected communication that had characterized enterprise operations. HR processes became self-service and auditable. Customer ordering moved from phone-based to digital. Field logistics gained real-time visibility where none had existed.

The most significant outcome was the shift in operational control — management could now see and act on workforce, order, and delivery data in real time rather than relying on end-of-day or end-of-week consolidation.
