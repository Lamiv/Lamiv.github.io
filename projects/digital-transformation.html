Digital Transformation Program

Led the enterprise-wide digitization of HR processes, customer-facing ecommerce, and field logistics operations for a large continuous manufacturing enterprise — replacing manual and paper-based operations with integrated, real-time digital workflows across SAP and custom platforms.

Problem

A large continuous manufacturing enterprise was running HR on paper-based and legacy workflows, selling through telephone-based ordering with no digital channel, and coordinating field logistics through manual dispatch. These processes were slow, error-prone, invisible to management, and increasingly inadequate as the business scaled. There was no unified digital layer connecting enterprise systems with field operations or customer channels.

Transformation Programs

Delivered three interdependent digital transformation programs in parallel — each replacing a legacy manual process with a fully integrated, real-time digital workflow.

👥 HR Transformation & Digitization

Led the HR digital transformation, replacing paper-based and legacy HR processes with integrated SAP SuccessFactors workflows across the full employee lifecycle — from onboarding to performance and payroll.

  • → SAP SuccessFactors implementation — core HR, onboarding, and performance management
  • → Digitization of HR records, approvals, and employee self-service workflows
  • → Integration with payroll and attendance management systems
  • → HR analytics dashboards surfacing workforce metrics and compliance data

🛍️ Ecommerce Platform × SAP S/4HANA Integration

Designed and architected the integration between a new ecommerce platform and SAP S/4HANA using modern integration patterns via SAP BTP and CPI. The solution was built around the operational realities of manufacturing — not just generic ecommerce.

What made this integration distinctive was its intelligence at the point of order: orders were accepted or queued based on live production capacity, not just inventory stock. The platform handled multiple dimensions of complexity — material stock, packing material stock, SKU variants, packaging types, and customer-specific preferences — all resolved in real time against S/4HANA's production and inventory modules.

  • → Capacity-driven order acceptance — orders validated against live production schedules
  • → Real-time stock visibility across material and packing material categories
  • → Multi-SKU and packaging type support with customer preference management
  • → Modern API-led integration using SAP BTP and CPI event-driven patterns

🚚 Logistics Digitization – Driver Mobile App

Replaced manual dispatch coordination with a purpose-built mobile app for field logistics teams, integrating directly with the enterprise dispatch and transport management system. Gave operations management real-time visibility into every truck, route, and delivery — eliminating the phone calls and paper logs that had been the norm.

  • → Real-time route optimization and scheduling pushed to driver mobile devices
  • → Live logistics visibility for operations and dispatch teams via web dashboard
  • → Reduced manual coordination effort and dispatch errors significantly
  • → Integration with SAP TM and dispatch management for end-to-end tracking
