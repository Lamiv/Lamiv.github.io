P Problem

Enterprise data was fragmented across departments including HR, sales, procurement, production, and logistics — with limited cross-functional visibility and slow, manual reporting cycles. Leaders lacked real-time insights to make proactive decisions, and yield optimization remained a largely reactive process. Process inefficiencies were compounding into significant operational and financial losses.

S Solution

Led a full-scale enterprise analytics transformation using SAP Analytics Cloud as the unified reporting and visualization platform. The program included:

  • → Enterprise data model design and ELT pipeline implementation for real-time data integration
  • → SAP Analytics Cloud dashboard suite covering all major business functions
  • → Predictive analytics models for yield optimization and production performance
  • → Process redesign to eliminate manual reporting steps and reduce error rates

D Key Dashboards

📈 Yield Optimization Dashboard

Tracks production yield across all processes using predictive analytics to identify optimization opportunities before losses occur. Enables proactive, data-driven production decisions resulting in $7M+ projected annual savings.

💰 Production Cost Dashboard

Breaks down cost per unit and per process, identifies cost drivers and inefficiencies, and provides real-time margin analysis to support profitability improvement strategies.

🛒 Procurement Analytics

Supplier performance tracking, spend analysis, and strategic sourcing insights. Enables procurement teams to negotiate better and manage supplier risk proactively.

🏭 Operations & Logistics Dashboard

Real-time tracking of production and dispatch performance, bottleneck identification, and process KPI monitoring across the full logistics chain.
