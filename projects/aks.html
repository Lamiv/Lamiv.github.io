Problem

A large continuous manufacturing enterprise was operating with fragmented data, siloed processes, and limited real-time visibility across production, procurement, HR, and logistics. Reporting relied on manual consolidation, decision-making lagged behind operational reality, and there was no unified digital layer connecting enterprise systems with field operations or customer-facing channels.

Transformation Scope

Led an end-to-end digital transformation program structured across five interconnected workstreams — each delivering measurable outcomes and building toward a fully intelligent enterprise.

📊 SAP Analytics Cloud – Enterprise Reporting

Implemented a unified analytics platform replacing fragmented, manual reporting across all business functions — production, procurement, HR, finance, and logistics.

📈 Yield Optimization Dashboard

Predictive analytics tracking production yield across all processes, enabling proactive intervention before losses occur. Outcome: $7M+ projected annual savings.

💰 Production Cost Dashboard

Cost-per-unit and process-level breakdown identifying inefficiencies and margin leakage in real time across the manufacturing line.

🛒 Procurement Analytics

Supplier performance tracking, spend analysis, and strategic sourcing insights enabling data-driven procurement negotiations.

🏭 Operations & Logistics Dashboard

Real-time production and dispatch tracking, bottleneck identification, and cross-functional process performance monitoring.

🛍️ Ecommerce Platform × SAP S/4HANA Integration

Designed and architected the integration between a new ecommerce platform and SAP S/4HANA using modern integration patterns via SAP BTP and CPI. The solution was built around the operational realities of manufacturing — not just generic ecommerce.

What made this integration distinctive was its intelligence at the point of order: orders were accepted or queued based on live production capacity, not just inventory stock. The platform handled multiple dimensions of complexity — material stock, packing material stock, SKU variants, packaging types, and customer-specific preferences — all resolved in real time against S/4HANA's production and inventory modules.

  • → Capacity-driven order acceptance — orders validated against live production schedules
  • → Real-time stock visibility across material and packing material categories
  • → Multi-SKU and packaging type support with customer preference management
  • → Modern API-led integration using SAP BTP and CPI event-driven patterns

👥 HR Transformation & Digitization

Led the HR digital transformation, replacing paper-based and legacy HR processes with integrated SAP SuccessFactors workflows across the employee lifecycle.

  • → SAP SuccessFactors implementation — core HR, onboarding, and performance management
  • → Digitization of HR records, approvals, and employee self-service workflows
  • → Integration with payroll and attendance management systems
  • → HR analytics dashboards surfacing workforce metrics and compliance data

🚚 Logistics Digitization – Driver Mobile App

Replaced manual dispatch coordination with a purpose-built mobile app for field logistics teams, integrating directly with the enterprise dispatch and transport management system.

  • → Real-time route optimization and scheduling
  • → Live logistics visibility for operations and dispatch teams
  • → Reduced manual coordination effort and dispatch errors
