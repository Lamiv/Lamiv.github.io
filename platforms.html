Flagship Platform

LEO – Enterprise AI Orchestration Platform

An AI-native orchestration layer designed to bring agentic intelligence into enterprise systems, particularly SAP landscapes. LEO introduces an agent layer that can reason, act, and interact with enterprise data and services.

The Problem

Traditional enterprise systems are process-driven but lack intelligence and adaptability. SAP landscapes, despite their data richness, operate on rigid workflow logic with no capacity to reason, prioritize, or act dynamically. Every exception requires human intervention. Every decision depends on someone pulling a report.

The Solution

LEO is an AI-native orchestration platform that sits between enterprise users and SAP systems. It introduces an agent layer capable of dynamic workflow execution, semantic understanding of enterprise data, and governed action across integrated business services.

ESG Intelligence Platform

Ecodrisil – ESG and Sustainability Intelligence

A generative AI platform for ESG reporting, sustainability insight generation, and compliance intelligence. Built to make sustainability data more accessible, actionable, and scalable for enterprise use.

The Problem

ESG reporting is fragmented across hundreds of documents, frameworks, and data sources. Organizations struggle to aggregate, interpret, and act on sustainability data at the pace and scale that stakeholders and regulators increasingly demand.

The Solution

Ecodrisil applies RAG pipelines, natural language querying, and document intelligence to ESG information. Users can ask sustainability questions in plain language, benchmark performance, and generate insight-driven reports — without manual data aggregation.

My Contribution

Contributed as a pro-bono AI architecture advisor — shaping the AI design, retrieval architecture, NLQ approach, and data layer strategy. The platform design reflects a systems-first approach to making ESG data useful at enterprise scale.
