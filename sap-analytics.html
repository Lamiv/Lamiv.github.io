SAP Analytics & Decision Intelligence

Analytics Built for Decisions, Not Just Reporting

Designed and implemented analytics solutions across manufacturing and operations, focusing on actionable insights rather than reporting. The goal at every stage: enable faster, better-informed business decisions.

Background

The Programme

This analytics programme was built to replace fragmented, manual reporting across a large manufacturing operation with an integrated SAP Analytics Cloud intelligence layer. The focus was not just dashboard delivery — it was about enabling real-time decision-making across production, procurement, logistics, and commercial operations.

Key design principles: every dashboard had to answer a specific business question, every metric had to be traceable to a decision, and every insight had to be accessible without technical support.

The four dashboards below are sample recreations of the live systems, built with anonymised and scaled data to preserve confidentiality. The analytics logic, layout, and decision frameworks are authentic.
